About Us

The University of North Georgia Computer Science Club was established in 2018 to bring together students majoring in CS, and those interested in the field with various skill levels. We provide a fun and safe enviroment to code and share knowledge. Our Meetings are located in the student center second floor, and they are every Tuesday and Thursday 1:30 to 2:15. A computer is not required, as there is a lab on the second floor in the locations of meetings. For more information, contact one of the officials through social media with the links below.

Members

      Interested in becoming a member of the Univeristy of North Georgia's Computer Science Club? If you are passionate about coding and like to be around others that feel the same way the UNG Computer Science club is for you. Somethings you should know is that membership is free, and we have bi-weekly meetings. We compete localy(on campus) and through out the state against fellow club members and in regional coding championships. You dont have to be the best programmer but being a member will make you become a better programer, through learning from other members as well as just asking for help if you need it. Being a member gives you access to an elite sect of UNG that only a few can proudly say they are apart of. So contact us through one of our social media links downbelow and we will help get you started as a member of the UNG Computer Science Club.
